01 · About

From research at Mila to shipping AI that moves the needle.

I’m a Machine Learning Engineer with 4+ years of experience building applied AI systems end-to-end — from search and recommendation to agentic LLM pipelines deployed on GCP and Azure. Today, I lead work on MAVEN, Instacart’s production agentic ranking system that orchestrates multiple models and external data sources across millions of daily queries.

Before Instacart, I built generative AI pipelines at Nissan (diffusion models, video anomaly detection) and researched RLHF for diffusion models at Mila under Prof. Narges Armanfard at McGill. My research appears at WACV 2025 (Top 10%), IEEE TPAMI, and IEEE/CAA Journal of Automatica Sinica.

I care about three things: models that actually ship, pipelines that stay reliable at scale, and evaluation that you can trust. I’m happiest somewhere between a research paper and a production runbook.
